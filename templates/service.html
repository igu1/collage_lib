Sullamussalam Science College Areekode

Career guidance corner

The college has set up a separate section in library exclusively for students preparing for competitive examinations like Civil Service UGC-NET, MBA, MCA and other tests conducted by PSC & UPSC There are more than thousand books in this section which is k open on all days except Sundays

E-resource centre

Access to National Library and Information Services Infrastructure for Scholarly Content (NLIST) resources is available using the username and password. More than one lakh e-resources are available through this programme. This provides access to e-resources to the students, researchers and faculty members from UGC-Info-net Consortium in world wide range.

Plagiarism checker

The college library has the facility to detect plagiarism using the software URKUND. The software helps to detect the possible plagiarism in a document and generates a report showing the similarties with other documents.

Reprographic service

A reprographic centre is functioning inside the library. This facility intended for copying various library resources and other outside resources.

OPAC Service

The computerized bibliographic details of books are available in the library through the Online Public Access Catalogue. It is a searching tool for a library document and its details.

Digital library

Our digital library is designed to provide convenient and seamless access to a wide range of academic materials, enriching the learning experience of our students, faculty, and staff. Embracing the ever-evolving digital landscape, our college's digital library stands as a beacon of academic excellence, empowering our community to embark on an exciting journey of discovery and intellectual growth.

Open resources

We believe in the power of knowledge sharing and the importance of open access to educational materials. In this section, you'll discover a wide array of open educational resources that are freely available for anyone to access and use. Embracing the spirit of openness and collaboration, our Open Resources section aims to supplement traditional course materials, promote lifelong learning, and support both students and educators in their academic journeys. We invite you to explore these valuable resources and tap into the wealth of knowledge they offer.
